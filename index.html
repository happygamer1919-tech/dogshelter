Who we are

Dalyan Dog is a volunteer-led shelter focused on rescuing and rehabilitating puppies. We provide safe housing, nutritious food, medical care, enrichment, and socialization.

Transparency and community are our backbone—every donation of time or supplies directly improves a puppy’s day and shapes a life that’s brighter than yesterday.

Rescue & care

Intake, vet checks, vaccines, treatment plans, and gentle daily routines.

Enrichment

Walks, playtime, confidence-building games, and calm, predictable spaces.

Community

Local volunteers, donated supplies, and supporters who keep tails wagging.
