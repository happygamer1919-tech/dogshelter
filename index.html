About the Shelter

Dalyan Dog Shelter is a volunteer-led rescue based in Dalyan, Turkey, dedicated to giving abandoned and mistreated dogs a second chance at life. We provide rehabilitation, medical care, and lifelong shelter for over 60 dogs—many of whom were once neglected or injured.

Our team works closely with local vets and volunteers to ensure each pup receives proper vaccinations, food, and love. With community donations, we maintain clean facilities and build safe sleeping areas.

Visitors are always welcome to volunteer, walk a dog, or simply spend time at the shelter. You can find us just outside Dalyan town, near the Ortaca–İztuzu road. Every visit, every shared story, and every donation directly helps keep tails wagging.

How you can help

  • Volunteer at the shelter
  • Foster a dog temporarily
  • Donate supplies (food, toys, meds)
  • Share our stories on social
  • Support monthly to cover basics
