About the Shelter

Volunteer-led rescue, rehabilitation, and lifelong care. Transparent and community-backed. We focus on safe housing, good food, medical care, enrichment, and socialization for dogs of all ages.

How you can help

Volunteer

Join us at the shelter: walks, cleaning, feeding, enrichment, and cuddles.

Foster

Give a temporary home to recover, socialize, and prepare for adoption or sanctuary life.

Donate & Share

Help cover food, meds, and vet bills — and share our stories on social media.

Want to help as a business sponsor or organize a fundraiser? We’d love to talk.
